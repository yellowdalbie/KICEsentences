## [Step 1] $\mathrm{P}(A^C)=2\mathrm{P}(A)$ 조건으로 $\mathrm{P}(A)$ 결정

- **Trigger**: [확률 대수 조건] $\mathrm{P}(A^C)=2\mathrm{P}(A)$
- **Action**: [CPT-STA-PRO-003] $1-\mathrm{P}(A)=2\mathrm{P}(A)$ → $3\mathrm{P}(A)=1$ → $\mathrm{P}(A)=\frac{1}{3}$
- **Result**: $\mathrm{P}(A)=\frac{1}{3}$

> **📝 해설 (Explanation)**
> $\mathrm{P}(A^C)=1-\mathrm{P}(A)=2\mathrm{P}(A)$ → $3\mathrm{P}(A)=1$ → $\mathrm{P}(A)=\frac{1}{3}$

## [Step 2] 배반사건 조건으로 $\mathrm{P}(B)$ 계산

- **Trigger**: [배반사건 및 전사건 조건] $A$, $B$ 배반, $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)=1$
- **Action**: [CPT-STA-PRO-002] $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)=\mathrm{P}(A)+\mathrm{P}(B)=1$ → $\mathrm{P}(B)=1-\frac{1}{3}=\frac{2}{3}$
- **Result**: $\mathrm{P}(B)=\frac{2}{3}$

> **📝 해설 (Explanation)**
> 배반사건: $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)=\mathrm{P}(A)+\mathrm{P}(B)=\frac{1}{3}+\mathrm{P}(B)=1$
> $\mathrm{P}(B)=\frac{2}{3}$
>
> 따라서 정답은 ④번입니다.
