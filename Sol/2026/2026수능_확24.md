## [Step 1] 곱셈정리로 계산

- **Trigger**: [조건부 확률 조건] $\mathrm{P}(A)=\frac{2}{5}$, $\mathrm{P}(B|A)=\frac{1}{4}$
- **Action**: [CPT-STA-PRO-006] $\mathrm{P}(A\cap B)=\mathrm{P}(A)\cdot\mathrm{P}(B|A)=\frac{2}{5}\cdot\frac{1}{4}=\frac{1}{10}$
- **Result**: $\mathrm{P}(A\cap B)=\frac{1}{10}$

> **📝 해설 (Explanation)**
> $\mathrm{P}(A\cap B)$ $=\mathrm{P}(B|A)\cdot\mathrm{P}(A)$ $=\frac{1}{4}\times\frac{2}{5}$ $=\frac{1}{10}$

## [Step 2] 덧셈정리로 계산

- **Trigger**: [전사건 조건] $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)=1$
- **Action**: [CPT-STA-PRO-002] $\mathrm{P}(A)+\mathrm{P}(B)-\mathrm{P}(A\cap B)=1$ → $\frac{2}{5}+\mathrm{P}(B)-\frac{1}{10}=1$
- **Result**: $\mathrm{P}(B)=1-\frac{2}{5}+\frac{1}{10}=\frac{7}{10}$

> **📝 해설 (Explanation)**
> $\frac{2}{5}+\mathrm{P}(B)-\frac{1}{10}=1$ → $\mathrm{P}(B)=1-\frac{4}{10}+\frac{1}{10}=\frac{7}{10}$
>
> 따라서 정답은 **①번**입니다.
