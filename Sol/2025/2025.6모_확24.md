## [Step 1] 여사건으로 $\mathrm{P}(A)$ 결정 후 $\mathrm{P}(B)$ 계산

- **Trigger**: [배반사건 및 여사건 조건] $\mathrm{P}(A^C)=\frac{5}{6}$, $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)=\frac{3}{4}$, $A$와 $B$ 배반
- **Action**: [CPT-STA-PRO-002] $\mathrm{P}(A)=1-\frac{5}{6}=\frac{1}{6}$, 배반이므로 $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)=\mathrm{P}(A)+\mathrm{P}(B)$: $\mathrm{P}(B)=\frac{3}{4}-\frac{1}{6}=\frac{7}{12}$
- **Result**: $\mathrm{P}(B)=\frac{7}{12}$

> **📝 해설 (Explanation)**
> $\mathrm{P}(A)=1-\frac{5}{6}=\frac{1}{6}$
> 배반사건: $\frac{3}{4}=\frac{1}{6}+\mathrm{P}(B)$ → $\mathrm{P}(B)=\frac{9}{12}-\frac{2}{12}=\frac{7}{12}$

## [Step 2] $\mathrm{P}(B^C)$ 계산

- **Trigger**: [여사건 확률 조건] $\mathrm{P}(B^C)=1-\mathrm{P}(B)$
- **Action**: [CPT-STA-PRO-003] $\mathrm{P}(B^C)=1-\frac{7}{12}=\frac{5}{12}$
- **Result**: $\mathrm{P}(B^C)=\frac{5}{12}$

> **📝 해설 (Explanation)**
> $\mathrm{P}(B^C)=1-\frac{7}{12}=\frac{5}{12}$
> 따라서 정답은 ②번입니다.
