## [Step 1] 독립 관계 확인 후 $\mathrm{P}(B)$ 결정

- **Trigger**: [조건부 확률 조건] $\mathrm{P}(A|B)=\mathrm{P}(A)=\frac{1}{2}$, $\mathrm{P}(A\cap B)=\frac{1}{5}$
- **Action**: [CPT-STA-PRO-005] $\mathrm{P}(A|B)=\mathrm{P}(A)$이므로 $A$, $B$ 독립. $\mathrm{P}(B)=\frac{\mathrm{P}(A\cap B)}{\mathrm{P}(A)}=\frac{1/5}{1/2}=\frac{2}{5}$
- **Result**: $\mathrm{P}(B)=\frac{2}{5}$

> **📝 해설 (Explanation)**
> $\mathrm{P}(A|B)=\mathrm{P}(A)$ → $A$, $B$ 독립
> $\mathrm{P}(A\cap B)=\mathrm{P}(A)\cdot\mathrm{P}(B)$: $\frac{1}{5}=\frac{1}{2}\cdot\mathrm{P}(B)$ → $\mathrm{P}(B)=\frac{2}{5}$

## [Step 2] 덧셈정리로 $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)$ 계산

- **Trigger**: [확률 덧셈 조건] $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)=\mathrm{P}(A)+\mathrm{P}(B)-\mathrm{P}(A\cap B)$
- **Action**: [CPT-STA-PRO-002] $\frac{1}{2}+\frac{2}{5}-\frac{1}{5}=\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{5}=\frac{5}{10}+\frac{2}{10}=\frac{7}{10}$
- **Result**: $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)=\frac{7}{10}$

> **📝 해설 (Explanation)**
> $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)=\frac{1}{2}+\frac{2}{5}-\frac{1}{5}=\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{5}=\frac{7}{10}$
>
> 따라서 정답은 ③번입니다.
