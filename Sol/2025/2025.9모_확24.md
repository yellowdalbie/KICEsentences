## [Step 1] 독립 조건으로 $\mathrm{P}(B)$ 결정

- **Trigger**: [사건의 독립 조건] $A$, $B$ 독립, $\mathrm{P}(A)=\frac{2}{3}$, $\mathrm{P}(A\cap B)=\frac{1}{6}$
- **Action**: [CPT-STA-PRO-005] 독립이면 $\mathrm{P}(A\cap B)=\mathrm{P}(A)\cdot\mathrm{P}(B)$: $\mathrm{P}(B)=\frac{1/6}{2/3}=\frac{1}{4}$
- **Result**: $\mathrm{P}(B)=\frac{1}{4}$

> **📝 해설 (Explanation)**
> $\mathrm{P}(A\cap B)=\mathrm{P}(A)\cdot\mathrm{P}(B)$ → $\frac{1}{6}=\frac{2}{3}\cdot\mathrm{P}(B)$ → $\mathrm{P}(B)=\frac{1}{4}$

## [Step 2] 덧셈정리로 $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)$ 계산

- **Trigger**: [확률 덧셈 조건] $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)=\mathrm{P}(A)+\mathrm{P}(B)-\mathrm{P}(A\cap B)$
- **Action**: [CPT-STA-PRO-002] $\frac{2}{3}+\frac{1}{4}-\frac{1}{6}=\frac{8}{12}+\frac{3}{12}-\frac{2}{12}=\frac{9}{12}=\frac{3}{4}$
- **Result**: $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)=\frac{3}{4}$

> **📝 해설 (Explanation)**
> $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)=\frac{2}{3}+\frac{1}{4}-\frac{1}{6}=\frac{8+3-2}{12}=\frac{9}{12}=\frac{3}{4}$
> 따라서 정답은 ①번입니다.
