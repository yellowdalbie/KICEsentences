## [Step 1] 표준화 변환으로 점 제품의 정규분포 확률을 산출하기

- **Trigger**: [정규분포 표준화 조건] $X\sim N(9,\,0.4^2)$, $\mathrm{P}(8.9\leq X\leq9.4)$
- **Action**: [12확통03-04] $Z_1=\frac{8.9-9}{0.4}=-0.25$, $Z_2=\frac{9.4-9}{0.4}=1$. $\mathrm{P}(-0.25\leq Z\leq1)=\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq0.25)+\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq1)$
- **Result**: $\mathrm{P}(A)=\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq0.25)+\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq1)$

> **📝 해설 (Explanation)**
> A 제품: 표준화 후 $\mathrm{P}(-0.25\leq Z\leq1)$ $=\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq0.25)+\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq1)$

## [Step 2] 점 제품 확률과 동치 조건으로 결정하기

- **Trigger**: [정규분포 표준화 조건] $Y\sim N(20,\,1^2)$, $\mathrm{P}(19\leq Y\leq k)=\mathrm{P}(A)$
- **Action**: [12확통03-04] $\mathrm{P}(-1\leq Z\leq(k-20))=\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq1)+\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq(k-20))$. 두 확률이 같으려면 $k-20=0.25$ → $k=20.25$
- **Result**: $k=20.25$

> **📝 해설 (Explanation)**
> B: $\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq1)+\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq(k-20))$ $=\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq0.25)+\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq1)$
> → $\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq(k-20))=\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq0.25)$ → $k-20=0.25$ → $k=20.25$
>
> 따라서 정답은 **④번**입니다.
