## [Step 1] 표준화 후 확률 계산

- **Trigger**: [정규분포 확률 조건] $X\sim N(180,20^2)$, $\mathrm{P}(190\leq X\leq210)$
- **Action**: [CPT-STA-STA-004] $z_1=\frac{190-180}{20}=0.5$, $z_2=\frac{210-180}{20}=1.5$. $\mathrm{P}(0.5\leq Z\leq1.5)=\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq1.5)-\mathrm{P}(0\leq Z\leq0.5)=0.4332-0.1915=0.2417$
- **Result**: $0.2417$

> **📝 해설 (Explanation)**
