## [Step 1] 조건부확률 계산

- **Trigger**: [조건부확률 조건] $\mathrm{P}(A)=\frac{7}{10}$, $\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)=\frac{9}{10}$, $\mathrm{P}(B^c|A^c)$
- **Action**: [CPT-STA-PRO-004] $\mathrm{P}(A^c)=\frac{3}{10}$. $\mathrm{P}(A^c\cap B^c)=1-\mathrm{P}(A\cup B)=\frac{1}{10}$. $\mathrm{P}(B^c|A^c)=\frac{P(A^c\cap B^c)}{P(A^c)}=\frac{1/10}{3/10}=\frac{1}{3}$
- **Result**: $\frac{1}{3}$

> **📝 해설 (Explanation)**
> $P(A^c\cap B^c)=1-9/10=1/10$. $P(B^c|A^c)=\frac{1/10}{3/10}=\frac{1}{3}$
>
> 따라서 정답은 ④번입니다.
