$$
\begin{array}{|c|c|c|c|c|}
\hline X & 0 & 1 & a & 합계 \\
\hline \mathrm{P}(X=x) & \frac{1}{10} & \frac{1}{2} & \frac{2}{5} & 1 \\
\hline
\end{array}
$$
$\sigma(X)=\mathrm{E}(X)$ 일 때, $\mathrm{E}\left(X^{2}\right)+\mathrm{E}(X)$ 의 값은? (단, $a>1$ ) [3점]
(1) 29
(2) 33
(3) 37
(4) 41
(5) 45
