$$\mathrm{P}\left(A^{C}\right)=\frac{5}{6}, \quad \mathrm{P}(A \cup B)=\frac{3}{4}$$

일 때, $\mathrm{P}\left(B^{C}\right)$ 의 값은? [3점]
(1) $\frac{3}{8}$
(2) $\frac{5}{12}$
(3) $\frac{11}{24}$
(4) $\frac{1}{2}$
(5) $\frac{13}{24}$
