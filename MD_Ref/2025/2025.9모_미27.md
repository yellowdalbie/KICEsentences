$$f(x)+f\left(\frac{1}{2} \sin x\right)=\sin x$$

를 만족시킬 때, $f^{\prime}(\pi)$ 의 값은? [3점]
(1) $-\frac{5}{6}$
(2) $-\frac{2}{3}$
(3) $-\frac{1}{2}$
(4) $-\frac{1}{3}$
(5) $-\frac{1}{6}$
